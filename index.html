About Opti-Sleep

This app is designed to help you pin point your problem areas in regards to sleep and to direct you towards optimal more satisfying sleep.

This app will be able to monitor whether you are awake or asleep and over time compile enough data to help you with the right tips and tools to help you make the best of your sleeping hours.

The most important reason to track your sleep is that sleep is good for your health, so you need to be sure you're getting enough of it.

Napping has recently been associated with lower coronary mortality, possibly due to reduced cardiovascular stress mediated by daytime sleep.

self-reported sleep duration is only moderately correlated with actual sleep time as it is typical for someone to report having slept 6 hours but only having actually having spent 3 hours in the REM state. We will help you ensure you are meeting your bodys requirements for an optimal day when you get out of bed.
